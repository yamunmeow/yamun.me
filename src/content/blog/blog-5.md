title: "What if Earth Was a Donut (With Sprinkles)?"
description: "Here's a poem for a change."
pubDate: 2026-03-15
image: "/images/blog/blog-5.webp"
---

> Sometimes, I wonder... 

> How would I balance on an earth 

> that was a donut? 

> Would it be black or brown 

> white or pink? 

> would it even matter so much though 

> Would it be flat? 

> If so, 

> would I be on the dough 

> or the void? 

> Would there be lines 

> dividing the dough? 

> How would I know 

> those beyond the void though? 

> Would it be a torus? 

> like a tire? 

> Where would it be headed? 

> Then I remember 

> it's a difficult question 

> to answer 

> For I do not know

> that even when 

> the earth is not a donut 

> (with sprinkles)
